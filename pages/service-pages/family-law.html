Family Law

Family law matters can be complex and emotionally challenging. We provide clear, practical advice to help you make informed decisions and move forward with confidence.

What we can help with:

  • Separation agreements
  • Divorce proceedings
  • Child custody and access
  • Maintenance arrangements
  • Domestic violence applications

Separation and Divorce

Navigating a separation or divorce can be one of the most challenging and emotional periods in a person’s life. Alongside the personal impact, there are often complex legal and financial matters that need to be addressed. Our solicitors provide clear, practical guidance to help you understand your legal rights and options from the outset.

We assist clients with separation agreements, judicial separation, and divorce proceedings, ensuring that every step is handled with care and professionalism. Our role is to explain the legal process clearly, outline the available options, and help you make informed decisions that protect your long-term interests.

Whenever possible, we aim to resolve matters efficiently and with as little conflict as possible. Many family law matters can be resolved through constructive negotiation or mediation, allowing both parties to reach workable agreements without unnecessary stress or delay. Where court proceedings become necessary, we provide strong and experienced representation to ensure that your position is properly presented and protected.

Above all, our priority is to offer reassurance, clarity, and reliable support during what can often be an uncertain and emotionally demanding time.

Child Custody and Access

When children are involved in a separation or divorce, their welfare and best interests must always remain the central focus. Decisions regarding guardianship, custody, and access arrangements can be sensitive and complex, particularly when parents are navigating difficult circumstances.

We advise clients on all aspects of child custody (guardianship), access arrangements, and parental rights. Our approach is focused on finding balanced and practical solutions that prioritise the wellbeing of the child while respecting the rights and responsibilities of both parents.

Where possible, we encourage parents to reach cooperative arrangements that provide stability and clarity for their children. We assist in drafting formal agreements that outline custody and access arrangements in a way that supports healthy relationships and consistent routines.

If disputes arise or agreements cannot be reached, we provide clear legal advice and representation throughout the process. Where required, we can represent you in court to ensure that your concerns are heard and that the child’s best interests are fully considered.

Maintenance and Financial Agreements

Financial arrangements are a key component of any separation or divorce, and they often require careful consideration to ensure fairness and long-term stability. Issues such as spousal maintenance, child maintenance, and the division of assets can have lasting implications for both parties.

Our solicitors provide clear advice on financial matters related to family law, helping clients understand their rights and obligations while working towards practical and sustainable agreements. We assist with negotiating financial settlements, reviewing financial disclosures, and ensuring that agreements accurately reflect the circumstances of both parties.

Where possible, we aim to resolve financial matters through constructive negotiation and mutual agreement. However, when agreements cannot be reached amicably, we provide strong legal representation and guide clients through the court process where necessary.

Our goal is to ensure that financial arrangements are handled transparently and fairly, allowing you to move forward with confidence and greater financial security.
