Employment Law

Employment issues can have a significant impact on your career or business. We provide straightforward legal advice to help you understand your rights and resolve workplace matters with confidence.

What we can help with:

  • Unfair dismissal claims
  • Employment contracts and policies
  • Workplace grievances and disputes
  • Redundancy and termination
  • Settlement agreements

Employment law can be complex and constantly evolving. We provide straightforward legal advice to both employees and employers, helping you understand your rights and obligations in the workplace.

Our team offers practical guidance on a wide range of employment matters, ensuring that issues are handled efficiently and in accordance with current legislation. Whether you are dealing with a workplace dispute, reviewing an employment contract, or navigating redundancy procedures, we aim to provide clear advice and reliable support throughout the process.

Workplace Disputes

Disagreements in the workplace can be stressful and difficult to resolve without professional guidance. We advise on a wide range of workplace disputes, including unfair dismissal, disciplinary procedures, workplace grievances, and allegations of misconduct.

Our approach focuses on resolving matters as efficiently as possible while protecting your legal position. We work with clients to understand the circumstances surrounding the dispute, explain the available options, and develop an appropriate strategy for moving forward.

Where necessary, we can represent you in negotiations or before relevant tribunals and courts, ensuring that your interests are properly presented and defended.

Contracts and Agreements

Clear and well-drafted contracts are essential to maintaining a strong and transparent employment relationship. We assist both employers and employees with reviewing and preparing employment contracts, ensuring that all terms are fair, legally compliant, and clearly understood.

Our services include advising on key contractual elements such as job responsibilities, compensation structures, notice periods, and workplace policies. We also provide guidance on restrictive covenants, confidentiality agreements, and other provisions designed to protect business interests and sensitive information.

By ensuring that agreements are carefully drafted and reviewed, we help reduce the likelihood of disputes arising in the future.

Redundancy and Termination

Changes within a business can sometimes lead to redundancy or the termination of employment. These processes must be handled carefully to ensure compliance with employment legislation and to avoid unnecessary disputes.

We provide guidance on redundancy procedures, termination of employment, and settlement agreements, helping both employers and employees understand their rights and obligations. Our focus is on ensuring that processes are carried out fairly, transparently, and in accordance with legal requirements.

Whether you are an employer managing organisational change or an employee facing redundancy, we offer clear, practical support to help you navigate the situation with confidence.
